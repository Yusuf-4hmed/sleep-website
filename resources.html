Why These Resources Are Useful

These resources were selected because they provide reliable and medically reviewed information about sleep and health. Websites such as the NHS and Sleep Foundation offer evidence-based advice that can help individuals understand sleep patterns, identify potential sleep problems, and improve their overall sleep quality.

Using trusted sources is important when learning about health topics, as inaccurate information can lead to poor habits or misunderstandings. These links support the information provided throughout this website and allow users to explore topics in more detail.
